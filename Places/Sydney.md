## Restaurants & bars

Central:

- Cantina OK – Super duper cool mezcal bar in the CBD
- Fabbrica Pasta Shop
- ==[Alberto's Lounge](https://www.albertoslounge.com/menu/)== – Always a great Italian cozy restaurant to go to
- Parlour Cucina (night coffee & intimate meals in State Theatre building)
- Opera Bar, a must-visit in the evening for the views of the city
- Restaurant Hubert (very cool French in an old undeground Chinese restaurant)
- Duke of Clarence (English-style pub, with old imported wood from dead London pubs)
- Konkara (great tiny Japanese restaurant)
- Menzies Bar & Shell House (upscale but cool restaurant)

North:

- Lee's Fortuna Court, legendary Chinese
- Peacock Gardens Chinese looks great too

East:

- Lox Stock – Good spot for bagels in Bondi, also halva
- Slow House have a bathhouse now, I'd like to check that out

Inner West:

- The Dock in Redfern (wed 8pm jazz nights)
- ==Ante==, a new sake/record bar
- Deadwax, record bar
- Silver's motel, a beautiful-looking cocktail bar
- Bearded Tit (very awesome queer bar)
- Ban Xeo Bar, awesome vietnamese in the Cannery, Rosebery
- Two chaps cafe
- Illi hill cafe
- Bob Hawke's Leisure Centre / Lucky Prawn (good Chinese)

East:

- Papi's tacos

Pyrmont & Darling Harbour:

- Blue-Eyed Dragon (very good Taiwanese restaurant)
- Lord Wolesley Hotel (sweet & old small neighbourhood pub)

## Beaches & Nature

- Bondi & Icebergs. Just go here.
- [Milk Beach](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Milk+Beach/@-33.8565645,151.264978,17z) – The bushland around this beach is beautiful. Ideal spot for a picnic and swim day
- Darling Point Reserve (cute little hidden picnic spot, great for new visitors)
