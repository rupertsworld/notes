**We chose this scenario deliberately.** In our research, we spoke with writers,  thought leaders, researchers, and investors who all described the same problem:  they're drowning in information. They're constantly taking in overwhelming amounts  from emails, feeds, articles, and messages, trying to quickly turn it into insight  and action. The result? They're not just struggling to navigate it all — they're  regularly missing important opportunities and insights because there's simply too  much to attend to.
