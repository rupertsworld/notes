# US-Iran war

#war #politics #middleeast

## Timeline

- **Feb 28–Mar 1** — US and Israel launch coordinated strikes on Iran. Israel codenames it Operation Roaring Lion, US calls it Operation Epic Fury. Targets include military facilities and senior officials, including Supreme Leader Khamenei's compound.
- **Mar 1–2** — Satellite imagery confirms damage to Iran's Natanz nuclear facility.
- **Mar 2** — Conflict spreads regionally. Hezbollah enters from Lebanon. A British air base in Cyprus is targeted.
- **Mar 3** — Iran retaliates against industrial and diplomatic targets across the Middle East. US closes embassies in Saudi Arabia and Kuwait, warns citizens to evacuate the region. Six US service members killed. Trump predicts the war could last "four weeks."
- **Mar 3** — Rubio publicly states the US struck preemptively because Israel was about to attack Iran independently, and the US anticipated Iranian retaliation against American forces. See [[Rubio says Israel forced US hand on Iran (Mar, 2026)]].
