# DMV

## Limited-term license renewal (E3 visa)

As a non-immigrant visa holder (E3), the California DL is a "limited term" license – it expires when the visa/I-94 expires, not on the standard 5-year cycle.

### What's required at renewal

- **Vision test** – required at every in-person renewal
- **Knowledge test** – not automatic. The renewal notice will say if it's required. Old rule was every 5th year; DMV language is now "if required"
- **Drive test** – not required unless specifically indicated on the DL record (per DMV REAL ID non-citizen page)
- **Documents** – updated legal presence docs (passport, visa, I-94)

### Renewal window

- Can renew 90 days before through 12 months after expiration
- No late fees for expired licenses
- Renewing within this window is straightforward; outside it may trigger additional requirements

### 2024 change

As of October 2024, written knowledge tests were eliminated for drivers 70+. This does not appear to broadly apply to all renewals – the "if required" language still applies for everyone else.
