# AI & thinking
#ai

How does AI change the way people think, engage with problems, and form original ideas?

## AI encourages shallowness

See [[AI makes you boring (Marginalia)]].

The way human beings tend to have original ideas is to immerse in a problem for a long period of time, which is something that flat out doesn't happen when LLMs do the thinking. You get shallow, surface-level ideas instead.

For example, when creating the `stash` project in early-2026, I didn't really learn anything about CRDTs and Automerge by the end of it.
