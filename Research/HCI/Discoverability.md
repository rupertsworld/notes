# Discoverability
#design #interfaces

Discoverability is the result of applying these five fundamental psychological principles (Norman 2013, 31):

- Affordances
- Signifiers
- Constraints
- Mappings
- Feedback

In addition to the conceptual model of the system.

## Affordances

An affordance is a relationship between the properties of an object and the capabilities of the agent that determine just how the object could possibly be used.

## Feedback

- Must be immediate
- Must be informative (don't just overload a single cryptic LED)
- Must be appropriate (too much can be irritating)

## Conceptual models

People will form these no matter what.
