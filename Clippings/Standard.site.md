# Standard.site
#web #protocols #products

https://standard.site

Coordination platform for long-form publishing on AT Protocol. Shared lexicons for publication metadata, document structure, and user connections. Leaves content formatting to individual platforms while standardizing structural elements.

Content portability (move between hosts without losing audience), community ownership (developers maintain the standard collectively), minimal governance (evolves through adoption not committees).

Verification via domain confirmation - publications add `.well-known` endpoint pointing to AT-URI record.
