# Red Queen's Race
#definitions #competition #metaphor

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Queen%27s_race

From Lewis Carroll's *Through the Looking-Glass* (1871). The Red Queen tells Alice: "Now, here, you see, it takes all the running you can do, to keep in the same place."

Used as a metaphor for situations where constant effort is required just to maintain position - in evolutionary biology (species must evolve to survive, not just to gain advantage), business (continuous innovation to stay competitive), and arms races.
