# Refine, AI research insights tool
#products #sensemaking #ai

https://www.refine.ink/

AI-powered research assistant that provides peer review feedback on academic papers. Analyzes manuscripts to identify errors, logical inconsistencies, and technical issues, delivering reviewer-grade insights in 20-40 minutes rather than months. Uploaded content is never used for model training.

## Cochrane review

https://www.grumpy-economist.com/p/refine

Economist John Cochrane tested Refine on an inflation manuscript and found the feedback exceptional — catching algebraic mistakes (a negative sign in a differential equation), identifying circular reasoning in core arguments, flagging unresolved theoretical tensions, and detecting ambiguities in transmission mechanisms. More concise and organized than typical peer reviews.

> "The comments it offered were on par with the best comments I've received on a paper in my entire academic career."

Cochrane also raised concerns about LLM influence on academic consensus — which theoretical frameworks get embedded in training data could shape professional doctrine across fields.
